Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots meetup for software engineers and has grown into one of the largest technology conferences in the world. Each year, it attracts thousands of attendees from around the globe.

Our Mission

At TechCon, we strive to inspire and empower the next generation of innovators by offering opportunities for learning, networking, and collaboration. We believe in the power of technology to transform society.
